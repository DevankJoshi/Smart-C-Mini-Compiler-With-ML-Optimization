# Quick Fix for "Can't find variable: execute"

## FASTEST FIX (Do This First)

### Chrome/Edge/Firefox:
1. Press **Ctrl+Shift+Delete** (Windows) or **Cmd+Shift+Delete** (Mac)
2. Clear cache and cookies
3. Go back to compiler
4. Press **Ctrl+F5** (Windows) or **Cmd+Shift+R** (Mac)
5. Try compiling again

### Safari:
1. Click **Develop** menu → **Empty Caches**
2. Close and reopen the page
3. Try compiling again
